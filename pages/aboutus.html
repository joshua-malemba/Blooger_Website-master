The Story Behind Our Vision

Meet Joshua Malemba, founder of JMServices, first class software engineering undergraduate and full time software developer on placement at Uniper.
During his first two years at university, Joshua quickly came to discover the various avenues of technology and how they are spearheading the future, whether that be globally, institutionally, and locally.
However, marked by the sparsity of ethnic minority involvement, he was inspired to initiate this service to educate younger disadvantaged students including ethnic minorities on involving themselves in the space.
After successful launch, the service quickly evolved, branching into bespoke software development and also private tutoring, mentoring and consulting.
