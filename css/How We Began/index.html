Meet The Founder

Meet Joshua Malemba, founder of JMServices, first class software engineering undergraduate and full time software developer on placement at Uniper. During his first two years at university, Joshua quickly came to discover the various avenues of technology and how they are spearheading the future, whether that be globally, institutionally, and locally. However, marked by the sparsity of ethnic minority involvement, coupled with the lack of technological awareness which local schools failed to solve during his life, he was inspired to initiate this service to educate younger disadvantaged students including ethnic minorities on involving themselves in the space. After successful launch, the service quickly evolved, branching into private tutoring, mentoring and consulting
